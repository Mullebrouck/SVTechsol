Solutions

Our product design and engineering solutions range from simple filter fan units to the most demanding custom front-end modules and mini-environments in the industry. Our standard approach to solving your filtration requirements begins with a comprehensive review of your process, air flow requirements, size restrictions and contamination sources. After this review S.V. Techsol integrates its expertise which includes ergonomic compatibility, maintainability, and automation interface in its designs and manufacturing. This process gives our customers the confidence in knowing their contamination control issues are resolved, freeing them to address the other challenges in process tool development and manufacturing.

S.V. Techsol specializes in:

  • Airflow Management
  • Vibration Control
  • Noise Control
  • Temperature Control
  • Humidity Control
  • Chemical Filtration
  • Pressure Regulation
  • Particle Management
  • CFD modeling
  • ESD Control

Whatever challenges you face, S.V. Techsol can support you in all facets of designing solutions to improve your processes. With our expertise and knowledge we will assist from conceptual design to OEM manufacturing.

We view quality as the value we deliver through our products and services, taking into account customers anticipated requirements as well as industry and market changes. We are dedicated to satisfying our customers by delivering solutions that meet requirements and exceed our customers’ expectations.
